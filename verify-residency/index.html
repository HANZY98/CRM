Residency Verification

First check that the applicant has submitted one of the following documents to verify their residency:

  • Current full or provisional paper driving licence.
  • A scanned paper copy of a bank statement, or bank or building society account opening confirmation letter.
  • A scanned paper copy of an original utility bill, or council tax bill.

Then check that the applicant’s residency document:

  • Belongs to the applicant; i.e. check the name and any other cross-referenceable information.
  • Indicates the applicant resides in the United Kingdom, or a Crown Dependency in the British Isles, excluding the Republic of Ireland.
  • If the applicant is applying for a Higher Technical Qualification (HTQ), they must reside in England.
  • Was issued within 12 months before the starting date of the applicant’s course.
