Criminality Verification

First check that the applicant has submitted one of the following documents to verify a past criminal conviction of theirs:

  • Basic Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) certificate.
  • Standard DBS certificate.
  • Enhanced DBS certificate.

Then check that the applicant’s DBS certificate:

  • Belongs to the applicant; i.e. check the name and any other cross-referenceable information.
  • Does not indicate the applicant received a past conviction involving cybersecurity-related offences.
  • Does not indicate fraud or financial crimes involving digital tools.
  • Does not indicate severe harassment, stalking, or threats conducted online.
  • Does not indicate misuse of online platforms.
This will attempt to proceed with the current Zoho CRM Blueprint transition.
