Reject Applicant

Reject the applicant if at any time their application indicates:

  • They are less than 18 years of age by their preferred starting date for their course.
  • They are not a resident of the UK.
  • Any of their documents are invalid, out of date, unrecognised, or do not correspond to the applicant.
  • Their criminal convictions indicate that recruitment with UniCourse may represent a threat to the safety and security of UniCourse and its staff.
  • They are not British and cannot verify their English-speaking proficiency if required.
  • They are unwilling to communicate, comply with requests, or accept offers made by UniCourse admissions staff or tutors necessary for progressing their application.
This will attempt to proceed with the current Zoho CRM Blueprint transition.
