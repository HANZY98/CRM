Identity Verification

First check that the applicant has submitted one of the following documents to verify their identity:

  • Passport
  • PASS card or CitizenCard
  • National Identity Card
  • Biometric Residence Permit

Then check that the applicant’s identification document:

  • Belongs to the applicant and fully corresponds with their application form
  • Check the name, sex, nationality, and age
  • Indicates the applicant will be 18 years of age by their preferred starting date
